Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Are new patients welcome?

Yes — new patients of all ages are always welcome. Just request an appointment online or give us a call to get started.

Do you offer emergency dental appointments?

We do. We keep room in our schedule for urgent visits and offer same‑day appointments whenever possible for issues like severe pain, swelling, broken teeth or a lost filling.

How often should I visit the dentist?

Most patients do well with a cleaning and exam every six months, though your needs may differ based on your oral health. We'll recommend a schedule that's right for you.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept many major dental insurance plans. Contact our office with your plan details and we'll help verify your specific coverage before your visit.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a photo ID, your insurance card, a list of any medications, and any recent dental records or X‑rays if you have them.

Do you offer options for anxious patients?

Yes. We offer comfort options including nitrous oxide and oral sedation, and our team takes the time to keep nervous patients relaxed and informed.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely. We provide children's dentistry and family care for toddlers, kids, teens, adults and seniors — all in one office.
