Welcome to the practice

We're glad you're here. Here's everything you need to know to make your first visit smooth, comfortable and easy.

Call to bookView all services

Your first visit is about getting to know you, understanding your goals, and building a plan that fits your needs and budget — at a relaxed, unhurried pace.

Your first visit, step by step

  1. Warm welcomeWe'll get you checked in and review your health history together.
  2. Comprehensive examA thorough look at your teeth and gums, with X-rays as needed.
  3. Your personalized planWe explain what we found and walk through options and estimates — no pressure, no surprises.

What to bring

  • Photo ID
  • Dental insurance card (if you have one)
  • List of current medications
  • Any recent dental records or X-rays

Comfort comes first

If dental visits make you anxious, you're in good company — and we're here to help. Let us know how you're feeling, and we'll go at your pace and explain what's happening before we begin.

Insurance

We accept many major dental insurance plans. See our insurance page, or just call with your plan details and we'll help you sort out your coverage.

We book by phone so we can match you with the right time. New patients welcome.
