About our practice

A family dental home in Troy

Square Lake Family Dentistry is a Troy dental practice led by Dr. Saif Hanna, welcoming patients of every age — one office for your whole family.

Call to bookView all services

We believe great dental care is built on trust, honesty and treating people the way we'd want our own families treated. That's the standard we hold ourselves to at every visit.

Local, independent and personal

Square Lake Family Dentistry is an independent, locally owned dental office. Being independent lets us slow down, get to know you, and recommend only the care you actually need.

Care for every stage of life

From a toddler's very first checkup to dentures and implants later in life, we provide comprehensive care under one roof. No bouncing between offices, no starting over with someone new — just the same familiar team looking after your whole family.
